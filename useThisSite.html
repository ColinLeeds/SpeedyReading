How to use this site

On the home page a passage of text will appear, with the first few words highlighted. Simply click Go and the highlighted area of text will move, until the end of the text is reached. To read the same passage of text again, simply click Go again.

To load a different passage of text simply click Change Text. If you want to stop reading a passage at any time, just click Stop.

Click 'Show controls' to show additional controls:

  • Text size - this adjusts the size of the text in the reading area.
  • Words in a group - this adjusts the number of words highlighted at one time.
  • Highlight time - this adjusts the amount of time a highlighted word group is displayed of.
  • Color Controls - these allow you to adjust text and background colors.
