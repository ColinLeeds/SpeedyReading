What is speed reading?

Speed reading is a technique which will allow you to read more quickly. But it's not just about being able to read fast, it's about reading fast while still understanding what you are reading. Speed reading has been around for a long time and has proven to be very effective.
If you'd like to know more about speed reading then take a look at the Wikipedia article.

How do I speed read?

To speed read you need to train your eyes to take in more than just a single word at a time, as you read. You need to train your eyes to take in groups of words. The more words your eyes can take in at a time, the faster you'll read. Also, the faster you can take in these groups of words, the faster you'll read.
